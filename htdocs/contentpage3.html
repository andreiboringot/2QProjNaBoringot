Safety Protocols Against COVID-19

The protocols that we have are there, so that they would be followed. Following these protocols makes us less vulnerable of catching this virus.

Keep yourself and others safe

Get vaccinated as soon as possible. Keep at least a 1 meter physical distance from others.

Wear your mask properly

Always make sure that your mask covers your nose, mouth, and chin.

Make your environment safer

Meet people outside. Outdoor gatherings are safer than indoor gatherings.

Keep good hygiene

Always clean and sanitize your hands especially when you just came from outside.
