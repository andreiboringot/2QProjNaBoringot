Benefits of Being Vaccinated

Getting vaccinated doesn't automatically mean that you are safe from the virus. When you catch the virus, the symptoms would be less contagious. Let us remember than even we are vaccinated already, we must not forget to follow the safety prtocols because there is still a chance for us to catch the virus.

The vaccine reduces your risk of infection

With the help of the vaccine you are less likely to get infected, but let us not ignore the fact that the vaccine doesn't have a hundred percent efficiency, so we must not forget to follow the minimum health and safety protocols.

The vaccine can help your unborn baby or newborn

Studies have found that expectant mothers who receive the COVID-19 vaccine create antibodies to the virus and pass those to their unborn baby through the placenta.

The vaccine protects against severe illness

This means that if we catch the virus, its symptoms wouldn't be that bad. We will most likely have a mild case of COVID-19.
